How We Started?

Our brand was first launched by Philip Rieck, a young entrepreneur seeking to make the world a greater place for the communities. He first started on February 2022 in his parent’s garage, his parents didn’t approve nor supported him in his journey. But once he started to get revenue, his parents started to believe in his stupidities and help him through his journey.

Why We Started?

We started to make the lives of our clients more accessible and easier than before by inventing and releasing products to facilitate their daily lives. These products range from many different difficulties or situations that Philip has encountered in his life and knows that other people out there have the same issues.

Furthermore, he also hates to do to repetitive tasks, so he likes to make machines or inventions to do it for him or use products to hide the issues away. He discussed his ideas with several hundred people, and they all agreed to him, so that’s why he started his journey. These products are also made from recycled material which helps our planet.

Future Plans

Our future plans are to release hundred of products for our customers to enjoy and facilitate their daily life. These products will produce joy for our customer as they can be lazy and joy their life.

We will be starting to hire people who are interested on working with us in the future, the positions will help us product and deliver our products faster then before allowing us to sell more and ensure that our customers are satisfied.
